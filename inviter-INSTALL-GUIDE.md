# Fast Start Inviter — Installation Guide

## What This Tool Does

Fast Start Inviter helps you launch your HBA business by automatically sending personalized invitation messages to your Facebook friends. Just select who you want to invite, and the tool sends 10 messages per day with natural timing (30-60 minute gaps) so you don't trigger Facebook's spam filters.

---

## Requirements

- Google Chrome browser (desktop)
- Active HBA membership
- Facebook account

---

## Installation Steps

### Step 1: Download the Extension

Download the ZIP file: **[fast-start-inviter.zip]**

*(Your upline or HBA will provide the download link)*

### Step 2: Unzip the File

**On Mac:**
1. Find `fast-start-inviter.zip` in your Downloads folder
2. Double-click it — a new folder appears automatically
3. ✅ You're done! Move to Step 3

**On Windows:**
1. Find `fast-start-inviter.zip` in your Downloads folder
2. **Right-click** the file → click **"Extract All..."**
3. Click **"Extract"** (leave the default location)
4. A new folder window opens — ✅ this is what you'll use in Step 5

⚠️ **Windows users:** Don't just double-click the ZIP file! That opens it like a folder but doesn't actually extract it. You MUST right-click → "Extract All"

### Step 3: Open Chrome Extensions

1. Open Google Chrome
2. Click the three dots menu (top right) → **Extensions** → **Manage Extensions**
3. Or type `chrome://extensions` in the address bar and press Enter

### Step 4: Enable Developer Mode

1. In the top right corner, toggle **Developer mode** ON
2. You should see new buttons appear: "Load unpacked", "Pack extension", etc.

### Step 5: Load the Extension

1. Click **"Load unpacked"**
2. Navigate to your Downloads folder
3. Find and select the `fast-start-inviter` folder (the one you extracted)
4. **Important:** Make sure you see files like `manifest.json` inside — if you see another folder, go INTO that folder first
5. Click **"Select"** (Mac) or **"Select Folder"** (Windows)
6. ✅ The extension appears with a 🚀 icon — you're installed!

### Step 6: Pin the Extension (Optional but Recommended)

1. Click the puzzle piece icon 🧩 in Chrome's toolbar (top right)
2. Find **Fast Start Inviter** and click the pin icon 📌
3. The rocket icon will now always be visible in your toolbar

## How to Use

### First Time Setup

1. Click the Fast Start Inviter icon (🚀) in your toolbar
2. Enter the email address you used for your HBA membership
3. Click **Continue** to verify your membership

### Loading Your Friends

1. Click **"Load My Friends"**
2. The extension will open Facebook and scroll through your friends list
3. Wait for it to finish (you'll see a count updating)
4. This may take 1-2 minutes depending on how many friends you have

### Selecting Who to Invite

1. After loading, you'll see your friends list with checkboxes
2. Friends who are already HBA members are marked with a green **"Already In HBA"** badge (unchecked by default)
3. Friends who have been invited before show a yellow **"Invited [date]"** badge
4. Check/uncheck friends you want to invite
5. Use **Select All** or **Deselect All** for quick selection

### Starting Your Campaign

1. Review the message preview at the top
2. Click **"Start Inviting"**
3. The extension will send ~10 messages per day
4. Messages are sent between 9am-8pm with 30-60 minute gaps
5. You can close Chrome — the campaign continues in the background!

### Managing Your Campaign

- **Pause:** Temporarily stop sending
- **Resume:** Continue where you left off
- **Cancel:** Stop the campaign entirely

## Tips for Success

1. **Don't use Facebook Messenger** while messages are being sent — let the tool do its thing
2. **Be patient** — the tool is intentionally slow to avoid Facebook flags
3. **Check your inbox** — respond promptly when friends reply!
4. **Get them your link** — when someone says "Yes", send them your HBA link

## Troubleshooting

**"This email is not associated with an active HBA membership"**
- Make sure you're using the exact email from your HBA account
- Your HBA subscription must be active

**"This email is already registered on another device"**
- Each email can only be used on one computer
- Contact support if you need to reset your device

**Friends aren't loading**
- Make sure you're logged into Facebook
- Try refreshing the Facebook page and clicking "Load My Friends" again

**Extension disappeared after Chrome update**
- Go to `chrome://extensions`
- Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder again

**"Manifest file is missing" or "Could not load manifest"**
- You selected the wrong folder. Look for the folder that has `manifest.json` directly inside it
- **Windows common mistake:** You opened the ZIP without extracting. Right-click the ZIP → "Extract All" first
- **Mac common mistake:** You selected the outer folder instead of the inner one. Navigate into the folder until you see `manifest.json`
