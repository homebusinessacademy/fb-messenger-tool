## How to Use

### First Time Setup

1. Click the Fast Start Inviter icon (ðŸš€) in your toolbar
2. Enter the email address you used for your HBA membership
3. Click **Continue** to verify your membership

### Loading Your Friends

1. Click **"Load My Friends"**
2. The extension will open Facebook and scroll through your friends list
3. Wait for it to finish (you'll see a count updating)
4. This may take 1-2 minutes depending on how many friends you have

### Selecting Who to Invite

1. After loading, you'll see your friends list with checkboxes
2. Friends who are already HBA members are marked with a green **"Already In HBA"** badge (unchecked by default)
3. Friends who have been invited before show a yellow **"Invited [date]"** badge
4. Check/uncheck friends you want to invite
5. Use **Select All** or **Deselect All** for quick selection

### Starting Your Campaign

1. Review the message preview at the top
2. Click **"Start Inviting"**
3. The extension will send ~10 messages per day
4. Messages are sent between 9am-8pm with 30-60 minute gaps
5. You can close Chrome â€” the campaign continues in the background!

### Managing Your Campaign

- **Pause:** Temporarily stop sending
- **Resume:** Continue where you left off
- **Cancel:** Stop the campaign entirely

## Troubleshooting

**"This email is not associated with an active HBA membership"**
- Make sure you're using the exact email from your HBA account
- Your HBA subscription must be active

**"This email is already registered on another device"**
- Each email can only be used on one computer
- Contact support if you need to reset your device

**Friends aren't loading**
- Make sure you're logged into Facebook
- Try refreshing the Facebook page and clicking "Load My Friends" again

**Extension disappeared after Chrome update**
- Go to `chrome://extensions`
- Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder again
